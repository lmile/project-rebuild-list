Traffic wireless vehicle detection solution

ROSIM WVD wireless vehicle detection sensor is designed for pursuit of high efficiency and low cost ITS traffic application systems. Wireless parking lots occupancy detector

WPSD wireless parking space detector, which is designed to sense outdoor parking lots occupancy information, integrates dual detection technology as well as a sophisticated algorithm to achieve high measurement accuracy. Based on uPower® wireless transceiver and ultra-small size design, WPSD sensors can be easily installed to the parking spaces in few minutes and keep no maintenance for many years.
